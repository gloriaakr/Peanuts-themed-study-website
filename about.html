Who Are We?

SnoopyStudy is your cozy study buddy! 🌟
We use magic (okay, AI) to turn your notes into flashcards, practice quizzes, and cute mini-videos to help you learn faster and cuter. Whether you're prepping for a test or just trying to stay organized, we’re here to keep you on track—with love, sass, and a little help from Woodstock 🐥.

This project was built by Gloria, a Computer Engineering student who loves tech, cartoons, and the chaos of learning something new.
